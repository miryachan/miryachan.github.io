ADOPTA

Pet Adoption App
  • Brand Identity
  • App

Development of a brandmark/logo/visual identity for a proposed fictional company.

Unfortunately many pets are euthanized in New Zealand because of overcrowded shelters. Adopting a pet is not a common practise and it is not very accessible and easy for busy people. Adopta is an adoption app which hopes to make adoption easier and a common practise in New Zealand.
